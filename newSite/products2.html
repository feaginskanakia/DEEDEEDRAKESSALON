SIMPLE TIPS

  • ♦ DO NOT USE CHEMICAL OR SYNTHETIC PRODUCTS TO HOLD YOUR HAIR TOGETHER “TO MAKE IT LOC” BEESWAX, LEMON JUICE,ETC…
  • ♦ DO NOT CONSTANTLY TWIST YOUR HAIR TO “MAKE IT LOC”.
  • ♦ ONCE THE LOCS ARE STARTED “LET THEM BE”.
  • ♦ AT LEAST TWICE A WEEK, MOISTURIZE YOUR SCALP AND HAIR.
  • ♦ DO NOT USE ANY PRODUCT ON YOUR HAIR EVERYDAY THAT INCLUDES OILS, GREASE OR SPRAY.
  • ♦ TIE YOUR HEAD UP EVERY NIGHT.
  • ♦ ENJOY YOUR LOCS, DON’T FOCUS ON THEM TOO MUCH!
  • ♦ DO NOT TRY TO CONTROL YOUR LOCS ALL THE TIME LET LET THEM DEVELOP FREELY.
  • ♦ GET A GOOD FOUNDATION’ START YOUR LOCS BY GOING TO A PROFESSIONAL LOCTICIAN.
  • ♦ CLEAN HAIR IS HEALTHY HAIR.
  • ♦ ENJOY THE MANY STAGES OF DEVELOPMENT OF YOUR LOCS.
